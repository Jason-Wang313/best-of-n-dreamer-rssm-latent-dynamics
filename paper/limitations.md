# Limitations

The evidence is controlled and CPU-scale. The RSSM model is intentionally small, and the main environments are hidden-mode toys. The added Gymnasium runs include lightweight toy-text stress tests and standard low-dimensional classic-control tasks, not broad RL validation. The repair scorers are diagnostic baselines, not a universal recipe. No full Dreamer benchmark, broad external RL suite, high-dimensional visual-control result, or real-world robot evaluation is included.
