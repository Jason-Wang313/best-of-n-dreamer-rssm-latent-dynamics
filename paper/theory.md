# Theory

The core measurement identity is the finite score-tie law in `docs/theory.md`. It converts a fixed empirical pool into exact selected utility curves for any `N`.

The law does not assume the score is correct. If score and executed utility are aligned, Best-of-N can help. If the score is aligned with imagined latent value but misaligned with executed utility in the upper tail, Best-of-N can expose the mismatch.
